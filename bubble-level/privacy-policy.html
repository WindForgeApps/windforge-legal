1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how WindForge LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information in connection with Bubble Level — Pro Precision (the “App”), available on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. We are committed to full transparency — including data collected automatically by third-party SDKs integrated into the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App itself does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The only data collection occurs through Google AdMob, which serves banner ads within the App. Here is a complete summary of all data associated with your use of this App:

Data Type Source Purpose Linked to Identity?
Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID) Google AdMob SDK Serving relevant banner ads Yes
Approximate location (IP-based) Google AdMob SDK Serving region-appropriate ads No
Calibration offset values App — local storage only Remembering your level calibration between sessions Never leaves your device

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, precise GPS location, contacts, photos, crash reports, or any usage analytics. There are no user accounts, no login, and no data synced to WindForge servers of any kind.

3. How Your Device Sensors Are Used

Bubble Level — Pro Precision uses your device’s built-in accelerometer to measure tilt and display a real-time bubble level indicator. All sensor data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to WindForge LLC or any third party. No sensor readings are stored beyond the current active session.

On iOS, the operating system will prompt you to grant motion sensor access when you first launch the App. If you deny this permission, the App will not be able to function. Regardless of your choice, no sensor data ever leaves your device.

4. Calibration Data

If you use the calibration feature, your calibration offset values are saved locally on your device using your operating system’s standard local storage (shared preferences). This data is never transmitted to WindForge LLC or any third party, and is not accessible remotely. It remains on your device until you uninstall the App or manually clear App data through your device settings.

5. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays small banner ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device’s Advertising ID and other non-personal signals — such as your approximate location derived from your IP address — to serve ads that may be relevant to your interests. This is standard industry practice for free, ad-supported apps.

You can opt out of interest-based advertising at any time using your device’s built-in settings:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → “Delete advertising ID” or “Opt out of Ads Personalization”
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → toggle off “Personalized Ads”

Opting out does not remove ads from the App but ensures they are not personalized based on your interests. To learn more about how Google uses data from ad-supported apps, visit policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

We do not sell your personal information. The only third-party service integrated into this App is Google AdMob. Any data collected by AdMob is governed exclusively by Google’s own privacy policy. WindForge LLC does not receive, store, or process that data on its own servers.

WindForge LLC does not store any personal data on its own servers. Advertising data collected by Google AdMob is managed and retained by Google per their own data retention policies.

Calibration data stored locally on your device is retained indefinitely until you uninstall the App or clear App data in your device settings.
