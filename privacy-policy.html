Privacy Policy

Bubble Level — Pro Precision  •  WindForge LLC

Ads Served via AdMob Analytics & Crash Reporting No Account Required

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how WindForge LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you use Bubble Level — Pro Precision (the “App”), available on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. We are committed to full transparency — including data collected automatically by third-party SDKs integrated into the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App itself does not directly collect personal information. However, integrated third-party SDKs collect certain data automatically. Here is a complete summary:

Data Type Source Purpose Linked to Identity?
Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID) Google AdMob SDK Serving relevant ads Yes
Approximate location (IP-based) Google AdMob SDK Serving region-appropriate ads No
App usage events (screens viewed, session length) Firebase Analytics Understanding how users interact with the App No
Crash logs and device state at time of crash Firebase Crashlytics Identifying and fixing App stability issues No
Purchase transaction records Google Play / Apple App Store Processing in-app purchases and restoring purchases Yes (by platform, not by WindForge)
Calibration offset values App (local storage only) Remembering your level calibration between sessions Never leaves your device

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, precise GPS location, contacts, or photos.

3. How Your Device Sensors Are Used

Bubble Level — Pro Precision uses your device’s built-in accelerometer to measure tilt and display a real-time bubble level. All sensor data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to WindForge LLC or any third party. No sensor readings are stored beyond the active session.

On iOS, the system will prompt you to allow motion sensor access. If you deny this permission, the App will not function correctly.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays banner ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device’s Advertising ID and other non-personal signals — such as app activity and approximate location — to serve ads relevant to your interests. This is standard practice for ad-supported apps.

You can opt out of interest-based advertising at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → “Delete advertising ID” or “Opt out of Ads Personalization”
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → toggle off “Personalized Ads”

You can permanently remove ads from the App via a one-time in-app purchase. After purchasing, no AdMob SDK requests are made and no advertising data is collected.

Review Google’s advertising privacy practices at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

5. Analytics and Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App — for example, which features are used most often and how long sessions last. This data is aggregated and is not linked to your identity.

We use Firebase Crashlytics to automatically detect and report App crashes. When a crash occurs, Crashlytics sends a report containing the device model, OS version, App version, and a stack trace. This does not include any personally identifiable information.

Both services are provided by Google LLC. Review Google’s privacy practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

6. In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time optional purchase to permanently remove ads, processed entirely by Google Play (Android) or the Apple App Store (iOS). WindForge LLC does not receive or store your payment card information. Your purchase history may be used to restore the purchase if you reinstall the App or switch devices.

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. Data collected by third-party SDKs is governed by those companies’ own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store: apple.com/legal/privacy

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request.

8. Data Retention

WindForge LLC does not store personal data on its own servers. Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics data is retained per Google’s standard retention schedules (typically 2–14 months). AdMob advertising data is managed by Google per their own retention policies.

Calibration data stored locally on your device is retained until you uninstall the App or clear App data in your device settings.

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to delete that information.

10. Your Rights (CCPA / GDPR)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of data associated with you. Because WindForge LLC does not directly store your personal data, most data subject requests should be directed to the relevant third-party providers listed in Section 7.

California residents: We do not sell personal information as defined under the CCPA. To submit a privacy request to WindForge LLC directly, use the contact information below.
